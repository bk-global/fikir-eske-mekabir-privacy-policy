Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

Fikir Eske Mekabir ("we," "our," or "us") collects the following information to provide you with a better reading experience:

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Reading Preferences: Your reading progress, last read chapter, and page position
  • Audio Preferences: Your last listened chapter and auto-play settings
  • Display Preferences: Your font size and theme preferences (light/dark mode)
  • Language Preferences: Your language choice (English or Amharic)
  • Bookmarks and Notes: Any bookmarks or notes you create while reading
  • Downloaded Content: Information about which chapters you've downloaded for offline reading

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage Analytics: How you interact with the app (features used, time spent reading)
  • Ad Data: Google AdMob collects anonymous analytics and ad interaction data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Remember your reading progress and preferences
  • Provide personalized reading experience
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Analyze app usage to make improvements

3. How We Store Your Information

Your information is stored:

  • Locally on your device using secure storage
  • By Google AdMob for advertising purposes (anonymous data only)

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • Google AdMob for advertising services (anonymous data only)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data through the app settings
  • Delete your data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app
  • Opt out of personalized ads through your device settings

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
